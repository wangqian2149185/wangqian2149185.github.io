Professional Summary

Scientific Builder Profile

I am a research scientist and AI research engineer with a Ph.D. in Biochemistry, an M.S. in Computer Science, and nearly a decade of empirical research experience in structural biology and biochemistry. My wet-lab background includes protein expression and purification, NMR spectroscopy, enzyme kinetics, molecular biology, mutagenesis, binding assays, and mechanistic studies of difficult protein systems. I have worked on projects involving antimicrobial proteins, metal binding, protein dynamics, and non-heme iron enzymes.

I build LLM-assisted scientific workflows, protein ML models, and structured evaluation pipelines for ambiguous research problems. My work sits between experimental science and AI systems. I care about careful hypothesis testing, reproducible experiments, model-behavior evaluation, and clear research communication.

I am especially interested in AI systems that help scientists work faster without losing rigor, evidence, or human judgment. This includes empirical AI safety, alignment science, and scientific AI tools that are useful, auditable, and grounded in real domain expertise.
